Origin

When the stars call,
we must answer

Humanity stands at the precipice of extinction. The Earth, once a cradle of civilization, grows cold and barren under waves of encroaching dust. Through the vast corridors of deep space, a faint hope stirs — a wormhole placed with impossible precision near Saturn's rings.

Our greatest explorers venture beyond the known, crossing the threshold between universes, carrying within them the singular force that defies physics itself — one that transcends gravity, light, and the cold mathematics of spacetime.
